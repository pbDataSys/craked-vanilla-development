Cracked Vanilla Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2025

Cracked Vanilla ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our website and subscription services related to our Minecraft server.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up or subscribe, we collect your email address and payment information via Stripe.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data such as your Minecraft username, IP address, and server activity for service delivery and moderation purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your subscription and upgrades
  • To maintain server performance and player experience
  • To respond to support requests or technical issues

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your data. We share your payment data only with our secure payment processor, Stripe, and only as needed to process transactions.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information. Payment details are processed securely through Stripe and are never stored on our servers.

5. Your Choices

You may contact us to update or delete your data. Canceling your subscription will stop future payments but may affect your server access and perks.

6. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: support@cracked-vanilla.com
